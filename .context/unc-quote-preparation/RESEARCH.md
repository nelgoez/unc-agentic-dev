## Negotiation Notes

- **Patricia's role**: She negotiates the political layer, we provide the numbers
- **Montes is key**: PSI must approve/bless the procurement. Make it easy for him.
- **Price in módulos**: Fits their existing framework. No new approval paths needed.
- **Three-currency visibility**: Módulos (primary), ARS (what they pay), USD (value reference)
- **Inflation protection**: Adjustment clause — if payment delayed >60 days, recalculate at current módulo value
- **Timing**: Good — they're expanding Campus Virtual (12 new programs) and have allocated tech budget
- **Pricing philosophy**: Affordable enough to close, not cheap enough to undervalue. The PSI just spent $7.7M on hardware — they can afford services.
